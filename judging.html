Judging Criteria

Teams who fit the following criteria will be eligible to present and win prizes.

Pitch Team Criteria:

Employees can submit a pitch before the main Hack to the Future event. Pitch submissions will be shortlisted by a panel of judges to demo their idea at the Hack kick off event, encouraging Hack teams to work on your idea during Hack to the Future.

  • At least 3 people on the team.
  • Exactly 1 minute to pitch.
  • Submit a completed 1 minute pitch deck and/or video by Wednesday, August 9, 11:00am ET.
  • There will be one prize awarded for Best Overall Pitch.

Hack Team Criteria:

  • At least 4 people from 4 different departments on the team.
  • Must build and present a coded prototype.
  • Can not enter/win in more than one category.
  • Exactly 2 minutes to demo.
  • Submit your team and demo details by Friday, August 11 at 8:00am ET

You can recruit as many people as you like for your team!

Departments are defined as:

  • Content (Acquisition, Researchers, Editors, Contributor Support)
  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Global Customer Care
  • Marketing (Creative, Content Marketing, SEO, Product Marketing, Sales Marketing, Media, CRM, Communications, Events)
  • Operations (Finance, Accounting, Legal, HR)
  • Product (Product Managers, UX)
  • Sales (AE, AM, SAE), Business Development
  • Tech (Developers, Engineers, Tech Ops, Infrastructure, QA, BI, IT, Delivery)

Register your team today on the Pitch registration page or Hack registration page. The deadline for Pitch team registration is Wednesday, August 9 at 11:00am ET, and the deadline for Hack team registration is Friday, August 11 at 8:00am ET.

Judging Categories

Hacks should be customer focused, improving their experience with Shutterstock as a seller or buyer. Hacks will judged broadly across our business units:

  • eCommerce Image
  • eCommerce Motion
  • Enterprise
  • Editorial
  • Webdam
  • Flashstock
  • We'll also have a special category this year for The Ultimate Plugin! By utilizing our Shutterstock APIs, we aim to drive product innovation outside the norm. In this category we are focused on extensibility: Wordpress Plugins, Extensions, or App gadgets/widgets - the tools that enable shortcuts in the creative lifecycle.

Each submission is only eligible to win in one category. Note: judges may move hacks to a different category they feel is more appropriate.

After Hack demos finish on Friday, August 11, judges will deliberate and then announce the winners via live stream the same day.

Prizes:

The winning teams will receive a prize (details TBD) and their hack/pitch will be considered for a sprint in 2017-18.
